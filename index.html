About Us

Liberation Lounge is a nonprofit dedicated to providing a safe, creative, and healing environment for survivors of domestic violence and underserved communities. Our mission is to empower individuals to reclaim their strength through therapeutic programs like rage rooms, art therapy, and sound therapy.

Our Programs

  • Rage Room: Break glassware in a safe space to release frustration and anger.
  • Art Therapy: Transform broken pieces into meaningful artwork as a form of self-expression.
  • Sound Therapy: Unleash emotion through music and sound in our "rave" rooms.
